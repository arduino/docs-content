<FeatureDescription>

The **Arduino® USB-C Power Supply (45W)** provides power delivery for USB-C devices with multiple voltage outputs. The adapter features interchangeable regional plugs for worldwide use.

</FeatureDescription>

<FeatureList>

  <Feature title="Power Output" image="power">
    Voltage outputs: 5 V, 9 V, 12 V, 15 V, and 20 V (up to 45 W maximum).
  </Feature>

  <Feature title="USB Power Delivery 3.0" image="usb">
    USB PD 3.0 and PPS support for automatic voltage negotiation.
  </Feature>

  <Feature title="Interchangeable Plugs" image="connection">
    Regional plugs for US, EU, and UK. Tool-free plug replacement.
  </Feature>

  <Feature title="Safety Protection" image="security">
    Over-current, short-circuit, over-voltage, and over-temperature protection with auto-recovery.
  </Feature>

</FeatureList>
