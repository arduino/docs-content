<FeatureDescription>

The **Arduino® USB-C Hub (8 in 1)** expands a single USB-C port into eight functional interfaces. The compact hub provides power delivery, USB data ports, HDMI video output, Ethernet connectivity, and card readers.

</FeatureDescription>

<FeatureList>

  <Feature title="Eight Ports" image="hw-pin">
    USB-C PD (65 W), USB-C Data, USB-A 3.0 (5 Gbps), USB-A 2.0, HDMI (4K @ 30 Hz), RJ45 Ethernet (100 Mbps), SD card reader, and TF card reader.
  </Feature>

  <Feature title="Video Output" image="display">
    HDMI output supports up to 4K resolution (3840 × 2160) at 30 Hz with audio pass-through.
  </Feature>

  <Feature title="Power Delivery" image="power">
    USB-C PD port supports up to 65 W power delivery with pass-through to connected devices.
  </Feature>

  <Feature title="Network Connectivity" image="wifi">
    RJ45 Ethernet port for wired network connection (10/100 Mbps).
  </Feature>

  <Feature title="Card Readers" image="file-icon">
    SD and TF (microSD) card readers support UHS-I and UHS-II formats. Both readers can be used simultaneously.
  </Feature>

</FeatureList>
