Build smarter and prototype faster with the Arduino Matter Bundle — easy way to create interoperable IoT devices using **[Matter®](https://csa-iot.org/all-solutions/matter/)**, the industry-backed standard by Apple, Google, Amazon, Home Assistant and others.

Our brand new Arduino Matter Discovery Bundle lets you quickly prototype Matter-over-Thread devices using open hardware.
The box includes a Matter-compatible Arduino Nano, 3 sensor/actuator modules, and a plug-and-play carrier.
The product comes with a 7-chapter curriculum to learn and build certified Matter nodes.
Perfect for DIY, training, rapid prototyping, and smart (or retrofitting) device integration.

## Key Benefits
- **Get your product ideas off the ground faster.** Matter library handles all the complex communication, so you can focus on innovation, know that training and tons of resources are available.
- **Work flawlessly with Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit, and beyond.** Forget vendor lock-in and make your devices compatible with tons of products worldwide.
- **Start debugging in no time leveraging the debugging over USB feature.**
