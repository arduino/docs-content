The Arduino® Starter Kit R4 is your hands-on introduction to the world of electronics and programming. This all-in-one kit includes everything you need to get started: a powerful [Arduino UNO R4 WiFi](../../hardware/uno-r4-wifi/), a wide selection of electronic components, and a detailed project book. Follow 14 foundational projects to learn the fundamentals, from making LEDs blink to controlling motors and reading sensors. Once you master the basics, online content will guide you in using the board's advanced features—like Wi-Fi®, Bluetooth®, and the 12x8 LED matrix—to build Internet of Things projects.
