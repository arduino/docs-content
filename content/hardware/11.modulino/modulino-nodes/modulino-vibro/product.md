A compact vibration motor module for adding haptic feedback and tactile alerts to your projects. Perfect for wearable devices, gaming controllers, notification systems, or interactive art installations. Compatible with Arduino UNO R4 WiFi or any Qwiic-enabled board, with simple I2C control for variable intensity vibration patterns.
