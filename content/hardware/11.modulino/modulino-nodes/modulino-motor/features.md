<FeatureDescription>
The Modulino® Motor module features the MAX22211 dual H-bridge driver for precise motor control. It handles two brushed DC motors, one bipolar stepper motor, or multiple solenoids and valves with up to 3.8A per channel. Connect via Qwiic cables for plug-and-play simplicity or use screw terminals for direct motor wiring with 5-24V power supply support.
</FeatureDescription>


<FeatureList>
<Feature title="Dual H-Bridge Driver" image="mcu">
Powered by the MAX22211, each of the two channels delivers up to 3.8A for driving small to medium-sized motors with independent directional control.
</Feature>

<Feature title="Wide Motor Voltage Range" image="power">
Operates with motor voltages from 5V to 24V, supporting a variety of motors while maintaining safe 3.3V logic control through separate power domains.
</Feature>

<Feature title="Versatile Motor Control" image="configurability">
Control two independent brushed DC motors, one bipolar stepper motor for precision positioning, or multiple solenoids and valves for pneumatic systems.
</Feature>

<Feature title="Qwiic Connectors" image="connection">
The Modulino® Motor connects to your Arduino board with Qwiic cables, letting you focus on your project without building complex motor driver circuits.
</Feature>

</FeatureList>
