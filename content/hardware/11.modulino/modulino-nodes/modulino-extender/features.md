<FeatureDescription>
The Modulino® Extender module features the LTC4311 I2C accelerator for long-distance communication. It transparently extends I2C bus range up to 30 meters using Cat5e or Cat6 cables, making it ideal for remote sensor networks and distributed installations. The module requires no configuration and works seamlessly with all Modulino® nodes and standard I2C devices.
</FeatureDescription>


<FeatureList>
<Feature title="Long-Distance Communication" image="connection">
Extend I2C communication up to 30 meters with Cat5e/Cat6 STP/FTP cables while maintaining signal integrity and reliability.
</Feature>
<Feature title="Transparent Operation" image="core">
No configuration required - the LTC4311 automatically accelerates I2C signals without addressing or special commands.
</Feature>

</FeatureList>
